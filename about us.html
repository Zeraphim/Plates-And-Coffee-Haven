ABOUT US

The little café referred to as Plates and Coffee Haven, often shortened to PNC, is in the Vito Cruz, Manila, offering not only coffee but also burgers and finger foods. With best-sellers like milky barako and baked penne, we aim to provide customers unique products only seen in this cafe.

A LITTLE HISTORY

"Cakes and Coffee"
This cafe was first named "Cakes and Coffee" back in 2018, but with several additions to the menu such as burgers, chicken wings, fries, and rice meals. It is later decided to change its name to "Plates and Coffee" to signify all the latest addition to the menu.
Growth
Plates and Coffee grew and made a reputation amongst LaSallians and other school institutions around Taft, Vito Cruz. It became one of the "tambayan" for the students.
Bounce Back vs the Pandemic
All of the other businesses became victims, and Plates and Coffee was no exception. The COVID-19 pandemic struck and made it difficult for the cafe to survive. However, this did not stop us from giving out and trying to help, especially to the frontliners against the pandemic.
